Caleb's Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Directions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the crushed garlic and sauté for about a minute, until fragrant and lightly browned.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and sauté until lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, whole black peppercorns, bay leaves, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat to medium-low, cover the pot, and let the chicken simmer in the sauce for about 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  6. Check the seasoning and adjust as needed. If you prefer a sweeter adobo, you can add 1-2 tablespoons of sugar or honey.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
  8. Enjoy the Adobo with your family.

    9. Note: You can adjust the amount of soy sauce and vinegar to your preference. Some people prefer a more vinegar-heavy adobo, while others prefer a saltier adobo. You can also add potatoes, hard-boiled eggs, or other ingredients to the pot for a more hearty meal.
