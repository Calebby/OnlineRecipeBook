Caleb's Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thigh, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginge
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet or pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© for about 5 minutes, until softened and lightly browned.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add the curry powder to the pot and stir to coat the onions and spices. Cook for another minute.
  5. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until lightly browned on all sides.
  6. Add the coconut milk and chicken broth to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the chicken simmer in the sauce for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  8. Check the seasoning and adjust as needed. If you prefer a spicier curry, you can add some red pepper flakes or cayenne pepper.
  9. Serve hot over steamed rice, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
