Caleb's Pork Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, boiled and chopped into small pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green chili peppers (siling haba), chopped
  • 1 red chili pepper (siling labuyo), chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges, for serving

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté for about 5 minutes, until softened and lightly browned.
  3. Add the minced garlic and chopped chili peppers to the pan and sauté for another minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped pork belly to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until lightly browned on all sides.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce and vinegar. Pour the mixture over the pork and stir to combine.
  6. Add the mayonnaise to the pan and stir to combine. Cook for another minute or two, until the mayonnaise is heated through.
  7. POptional: Push the pork to one side of the pan and crack the egg into the empty space. Scramble the egg until cooked and then mix it into the pork mixture.
  8. Serve hot with calamansi or lemon wedges on the side.
